Mission

Our Mission is to reach the WORLD music

We are passionate about uplifting others and becoming a source of Joy to those we interract with.

As Christians, we believe that music "is one of the most effective means of impressing the heart with spiritual truth. As a part of religious service, singing is as much an act of worship as is prayer. At its purest and best, music lifts our beings into the very presence of God." (Education, p. 168).

"Music, when not abused, is a great blessing; but when it is put to a wrong use, it is a terrible curse." Testimonies, vol. 1, p. 497. “Rightly employed, . . . [music] is a precious gift of God, designed to uplift the thoughts to high and noble themes, to inspire and elevate the souls.” Education, p. 167.
